4. **Guaranteed Money:** $28 million

6. **Performance Incentives:**

7. **Protective Clauses:**

- This proposal strikes a balance between competitiveness and fiscal responsibility by setting a clear structure for guaranteed payments and incentivizing performance metrics that align with team objectives.

- Reflects comparable market contracts, offering competitive terms that consider player's dual-role utility and market positioning.

**III. Implementation Timeline and Communication Strategy**

**Implementation Steps:**
1. **Contract Drafting (Week 1):** Finalize terms and conditions into a contract draft, integrating input from player’s legal team and Raven’s management.
   
2. **Presentation to Stakeholders (Week 2):** Host a meeting with Ravens management and player representatives to review terms, ensuring alignment with strategic team goals and financial strategy.
   
3. **Contractual Adjustments (Week 3):** Conduct any necessary revisions to address feedback or negotiation pivots.
   
4. **Final Approval & League Submission (Week 4):** Secure final internal approvals and submit the officially signed contract to the league office.

5. **Public Announcement (End of Week 4):** Coordinate a joint press release through the Ravens and player's publicists, highlighting the player’s future contributions and strategic fit within the team.

**Communication Strategies:**
- **Internal Communication:** Ensure all departments involved (cap management, PR, legal) are briefed on the strategic importance of retaining the player and the implications on fiscal planning.
   
- **External Communication:** Position the contract announcement as a strategic commitment to championship aspirations. Highlight the player's impact through community engagement and franchise value enhancement.

---

**IV. Fallback Scenarios**

**If Primary Deal Fails:**
1. **Plan B Negotiations:** Prepare an alternative two-year contract offering marginally lower annual earnings but higher immediate guarantees to potentially attract swift acceptance.
   
2. **Explore Trade Options:** Consider potential trades should negotiations stall, ensuring mutual benefit and maintaining roster balance.
