- Align the contract announcement with other significant team news, such as roster moves or organizational developments, to maximize media coverage and fan interest.
- Develop a cohesive voice and tone across all communications, ensuring consistency in messaging from top executives to coaches and the athlete themselves.

**4. Crisis Communication Plan:**  
- **Potential Criticisms:** Respond promptly to criticisms regarding contract size by emphasizing the player’s consistent performance metrics, leadership qualities, and alignment with market trends for WR contracts.
- **Value Proposition:** Highlight how the investment in [Player's Name] is intended to drive team success, bolster fan experience, and maintain franchise value.
- **Transparency and Engagement:** Encourage transparency by inviting discussions with community and media, demonstrating the decision’s alignment with franchise objectives and long-term strategies.

**5. Media Training:**  
- Ensure that all representatives, including [Player’s Name], are media-trained to handle questions effectively, focusing on team ethos, shared aspirations, and commitment to success.

**6. Fan Response Handling:**  
- Monitor fan sentiment on social media platforms and engage with positive feedback while addressing concerns. Use feedback to adjust communication strategies if needed.

**Conclusion:**  
By methodically implementing this strategy, the Tennessee Titans can maximize media impact, engage fans positively, and reinforce brand strength, ultimately supporting the franchise's ambitions and ensuring alignment with both sporting and business goals.
